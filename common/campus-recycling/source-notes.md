# Campus Recycling source notes

## Data provenance
- Source file: `campus-waste-data-2023-2024-simulated.csv`
- Period: January 2023 to December 2024 (24 monthly records)
- Metrics: landfill tons, recycling tons, compost tons, total tons, diversion rate, contamination rate

## Chart provenance
- `charts/diversion-rate-trend.png` generated from monthly diversion rate values.
- `charts/waste-stream-annual-totals.png` generated from annual stream totals.
- `charts/contamination-trend.png` generated from monthly contamination values.

## Evidence summary from simulated dataset
- Diversion rate improved from **49.2% (2023)** to **56.9% (2024)**.
- Landfill tonnage decreased from **930.0** to **807.5** tons.
- Mean contamination rate dropped from **17.4%** to **14.2%**.

## Evidence-based recommendations used in Episode 05
1. Standardize tri-bin stations with consistent visual guidance in high-traffic locations.
2. Run monthly contamination-focused communication prompts and examples.
3. Publish building-level waste snapshots to reinforce behavior change loops.

## Limitations
- This is simulated data for training and demonstration only.
- Values simplify real operational variance and should not be used for policy decisions without real audit validation.
