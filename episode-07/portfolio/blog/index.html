Blog

I made this blog to share what I am learning while building projects as a first-generation college student. I wanted one place to document what worked, what did not, and what other students can actually use.

  • I write about lessons from real project work, not polished highlight reels.
  • I focus on clear, practical takeaways another student can try right away.
  • I use this space to keep track of patterns that are helping me improve.
